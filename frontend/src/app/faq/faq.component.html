Ingrédients et Produits :

Dracoss Tap Water Filters are designed with safety as our highest priority. The filters are composed of materials that are safe for contact with drinking water. Furthermore, they effectively remove impurities and harmful contaminants from your tap water. However, they should be used and maintained as per the instructions provided to ensure their efficacy. If you have a specific health concern or condition, it's always a good idea to consult with your healthcare provider.

Dracoss filters are designed to remove a wide range of common contaminants found in tap water, including chlorine, heavy metals such as lead, and microbial contaminants like bacteria. Our filters use advanced filtration technologies to ensure that your water is safe and healthy for consumption. However, it's important to note that while our filters are highly effective, no filter can remove 100% of all possible contaminants, especially those at very low levels. For contaminants like certain industrial chemicals or pharmaceuticals, which might be present in some water sources, specific filtration methods may be required. If you're concerned about a specific contaminant in your local water supply, we recommend you get your water tested. Once you have the results, our customer service team would be more than happy to help you understand whether our filters are the right solution for you.

At Dracoss, ensuring the quality of our filtered water is a top priority. Our filtration system has been thoroughly tested and certified to meet CE standards, an internationally recognized certification that ensures the safety, health, and environmental protection standards of products sold within the European Economic Area (EEA). The CE certification means our filters have passed stringent quality control checks and have been found to effectively reduce contaminants commonly found in tap water. This includes substances like chlorine, heavy metals, and various microorganisms, making the water safer and better tasting. In addition, we have a robust quality assurance process in place to regularly monitor and test our products. This includes regular third-party lab testing to verify our filters' performance and ensure they continue to meet or exceed the high standards we've set. It's our commitment at Dracoss to deliver safe, clean, and high-quality drinking water to all our customers. We're confident in the effectiveness of our filters and stand by our certification and testing processes.

Sécurité et Allergènes :

Yes, we have designed our filters to be environmentally friendly. The plastic components of our filter are recyclable, and the activated carbon used in the filter is biodegradable. We encourage users to separate these components and dispose of them correctly at the end of the filter's lifespan.
